Teaching Method and Learning Materials

Expert Teaching,
Proven Results

We work with a curriculum specifically developed for this target group by the University of Groningen. This method consists of four e-books and covers all language levels from A1 through C1.

In addition to the structured core curriculum, we provide supplementary support materials. Students receive, amongst other, clear structure sheets in which specific aspects of Dutch grammar, such as verb conjugations, are explained in a concise and accessible way.

We also offer additional practice materials, allowing students to independently deepen and strengthen their knowledge.

  • ✓ Structured curriculum: 4 e-books, A1 through C1
  • ✓ Clear grammar structure sheets included
  • ✓ NT2 exam preparation, 99.7% pass rate
  • ✓ Periodic testing & progress evaluations
