Specialised Experience

Especially for South Africans

We have extensive experience teaching Dutch to South Africans. Our tutors know exactly what to focus on for Afrikaans and English speakers.

English

We have extensive experience teaching Dutch to South Africans whose first languages are Afrikaans and/or English.

Our tutors understand which aspects of Dutch are less relevant for South Africans and which require extra attention for this specific group. Reading and listening are often relatively easy for Afrikaans speakers, while writing and speaking can be much more challenging.

We offer a wide range of tips tailored to this group, including guidance on correct pronunciation of Dutch sounds.

Afrikaans

Ons het uitgebreide ervaring met die onderrig van Nederlands aan Suid-Afrikaners wie se eerste tale Afrikaans of Engels is.

Ons tutors weet dus watter aspekte van die Nederlandse taal minder relevant is vir Suid-Afrikaners, en watter meer aandag vereis vir hierdie spesiale groep. Lees en luister is dikwels maklik vir Afrikaanssprekendes, terwyl skryf en praat aansienlike uitdagings bied.

Ons het allerhande wenke gereed vir hierdie groep, ook wanneer dit kom by die korrekte uitspraak van Nederlandse klanke.

Key insight for Afrikaans speakers

Reading & Listening vs. Writing & Speaking

For Afrikaans speakers, reading and listening in Dutch are often surprisingly accessible — but writing and speaking require dedicated, targeted practice. Our tutors adjust the curriculum accordingly, from day one.
