Key insight for Afrikaans speakers

For Afrikaans speakers, reading and listening in Dutch are often surprisingly accessible — but writing and speaking require dedicated, targeted practice. Our tutors adjust the curriculum accordingly, from day one.

Tailored Curriculum

What We Focus On for You

Because we know your starting point, we can skip what you don’t need and concentrate on what matters most.

🧩

Afrikaans–Dutch similarities

You already have a significant vocabulary advantage. We build on your Afrikaans foundation from the very first lesson, making progress feel natural and fast.

🗣️

Speaking confidence

Spoken Dutch differs considerably from Afrikaans. We dedicate targeted time to fluency, rhythm, and natural conversational flow so you sound confident, not translated.

✍️

Writing accuracy

Dutch spelling, grammar, and sentence structure diverge from Afrikaans in important ways. We focus on the rules that trip up South Africans most — efficiently.

🔊

Dutch pronunciation

The Dutch “g”, long vowels, and diphthongs are common stumbling blocks. We provide targeted pronunciation exercises designed specifically for South African speakers.

🇳🇱

Dutch workplace & culture

Understanding Dutch directness, social norms, and professional communication makes integration smoother. We weave culture into every lesson.

🤝

One dedicated tutor

You work with one tutor throughout your programme. They get to know your background, track your progress, and tailor every lesson to where you are right now.
