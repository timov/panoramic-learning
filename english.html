About the Programme

Private English for ESOL Learners

Panoramic Learning provides private English lessons through Google Meet for intermediate and highly educated learners.

We have developed a dynamic English course specifically for ESOL learners (English for Speakers of Other Languages). Our programme is designed to rebuild your confidence and help you communicate effectively in real-life situations. Our tutors are native English speakers and know about the English spoken in the different regions in the world.

  • ✓ Private one-on-one lessons via Google Meet
  • ✓ Native English-speaking tutors
  • ✓ Knowledge of English across different regions worldwide
  • ✓ Designed for intermediate and highly educated learners
  • ✓ Focus on real-life confidence and communication
