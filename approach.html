How We Teach

Private, Personal, Flexible

Every element of our lessons is designed around one goal: your progress, at your pace.

🖥️

Private lessons via Google Meet

One-on-one video sessions in a virtual classroom. No commuting, no classrooms, just you and your dedicated tutor.

⏱️

55 minutes per lesson

Each lesson is carefully structured for maximum focus. Long enough to make real progress, concise enough to stay energised.

📅

Once or twice per week

Choose a learning rhythm that fits your life. Weekly for steady progress, twice weekly for faster advancement.

🕐

Flexible scheduling

Available any day between 5:00 and 24:00 CET. Our global team spans time zones so there is always a slot that works for you.

👤

One tutor, every time

You work with the same dedicated tutor session after session. They know your progress, your goals, and your learning style.

📚

Solid curriculum

Four e-books developed by the University of Groningen for intermediate and highly educated professionals. Covers A1 through C1.

🎯

Personalised guidance

Your tutor customises every lesson to your level, goals, and schedule. No two students follow exactly the same path.

📊

Periodic testing

Regular assessments track your progress, keep you on target, and give you clear milestones to celebrate along the way.

💑

Partners join for free

Registered partners can join lessons without additional cost, making it easy and affordable for couples to learn together.
